Don't be the Odd One Out

Our machine-learning pipeline for healthcare will revolutionize the planning, the treatment, and the approach to modern medicine. Our approach will optimize the interactions betweeen patients and their treatment teams, enabling all participants to see their data in a safe and protected environment. By 24/7 visibility of health-metrics, patients can be assured that their data is continuously being optimized by machine-learning algorithms and their care-teams to create a personalized health-plan that benefits their particular situation.

Our pipeline uses a private blockchain to maximally protect each individuals health data. This private blockchain supports the individual in providing them a unified framework for collecting all of their lab, physician and personally generated health metrics. Each individual user can dynamically determine when to share data and with whom. The medical care teams can, with the users permission, add data to the private blockchain, as well as add treatment recommendations. A continuously operating machine-learning framework provides guidance to the individual patients and the careteam at all times. In parallel the patients and the careteams have access to a public marketplace, the public blockchain, where DaiWare Tokens can be used within Smart Contracts for healthcare.

Streaming Health Metrics

Health metrics should be available 24/7 to both patients and care-providers. Our frameowork supports the ability for both patients and care-team providers to update the patient's blockchain in real-time. The resulting stream of clinically actionable information can be used to make meaningful medical decisions based on validated data.

Real-time Decision Support

Machine learning algorithms are at their best with large amounts of data. By enabling all of your health metrics to be defined within a private blockchain and by supporting our large cloud-backed infrastructure, the benefits of machine learning are applied to enable population and personalized medicine to be practiced at the very highest level.
